Common Cold:
Symptoms include runny nose, sore throat, cough, mild fever. Treatment involves rest, hydration, and over-the-counter medications.

Flu (Influenza):
Symptoms include high fever, body aches, fatigue, cough. Treatment includes rest, fluids, antiviral medications if prescribed early.

Diabetes:
A chronic condition affecting blood sugar levels. Types include Type 1 and Type 2. Managed through diet, exercise, and medication.

Hypertension:
High blood pressure condition. Risk factors include age, obesity, high sodium intake. Managed through lifestyle changes and medications.

COVID-19:
Viral infection affecting respiratory system. Symptoms include fever, cough, loss of taste/smell. Prevention includes vaccination and hygiene measures.

Heart Disease:
Cardiovascular conditions affecting heart function. Risk factors include smoking, high cholesterol, obesity. Prevention includes healthy diet and exercise.
